Televisions & Energy Use

In Australia, modern LED and OLED TVs use significantly less power than older plasma models. Choosing a 6-star rated TV can save households up to $100 per year on electricity bills.

LED TVs

Efficient and affordable, consuming 30–70 watts depending on size.

OLED TVs

Premium displays with deeper blacks, typically using 40–90 watts.

Plasma TVs

Older models can use up to 300 watts, making them costly to run.
